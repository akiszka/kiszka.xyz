## Design Context

**Audience**: Tech peers, founders, engineers, recruiters — people who recognize craft.
**Personality**: Playful, precise, grounded. The desk metaphor invites interaction; the typography and layout are disciplined.
**Theme**: Light (warm cream). Analog material language — paper, ink, tape, hard shadows.
**Core statement**: "I find beauty in simplicity."

**Anti-references**: Developer portfolio tropes (terminal themes, typed text), startup landing pages (gradient heroes, metric grids), generic AI output (cyan-on-dark, glassmorphism, gradient text).

**Design principles**:
1. Craft is the message — polish rewards close inspection
2. Physical over digital — interactions feel like moving real objects
3. Restraint is playfulness — one mechanic executed well beats five mediocre ones
4. Warmth through material — cream-and-ink palette, hard shadows, serif headlines
5. Simplicity is earned — every element present is necessary and considered
